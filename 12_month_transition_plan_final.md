# 12-Month Career Transition Plan
## Finance + Qlik Sense → Remote-Friendly Data/Analytics Engineer + AI-Powered Analytics

Maps 12-month roadmap to target roles, income bands, and countries (UK, Ireland, Netherlands, Germany, Switzerland, Dubai, Singapore, Canada, Australia, Bermuda, Remote US).

---

## Overall Goal

Build a remote-friendly **data/analytics engineering + AI-powered analytics** profile over 12–24 months:
- Keep finance + Qlik Sense as leverage
- Build Python + SQL + cloud + LLMs
- Open doors to cybersecurity later

---

## Target Role Ladder

| Level | Roles | Full-time (UK/London) | Freelance (UK/Europe) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundation | Data Analyst, BI Analyst, Reporting Analyst | £40k–60k | £250–400/day |
| Transition | BI Developer, Reporting Engineer, Data Operations Engineer | £55k–75k | £350–500/day |
| Core Target | Analytics Engineer, Data Analytics Engineer | £70k–95k | £450–600/day |
| Higher-Value | Data Engineer, Cloud-heavy Analytics Engineer | £80k–110k+ | £500–700/day |
| Premium | AI Solutions Engineer, AI-powered Analytics Engineer, Data Product Engineer | £90k–120k+ | £600–800+/day |

---

## Key Markets Beyond the UK

### UK / London
- Best short-term market (location + language)
- Target: Analytics Engineer, BI Developer, Reporting Engineer, Data Operations Engineer

### Ireland
- Strong English-speaking market, multinational tech firms
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, BI Engineer, AI Solutions Engineer

### Netherlands
- Strong analytics data market (~€103K total comp in Amsterdam)
- Good English, hybrid/remote-friendly
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, Analytics Product roles, BI/Reporting Engineer

### Germany
- Large market, Berlin ~€77K–100K total comp
- Good scale of opportunity and engineering depth
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, Reporting/Data Platform roles, Cloud data roles

### Switzerland
- Highest salary market in Europe
- Harder entry, expects strong experience or local fit
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, AI-enabled analytics specialist

### Dubai / UAE
- **0% personal income tax** – gross = take-home
- Data Analyst: AED 12K–18K (junior) to AED 28K–40K+ (senior) per month, tax-free
- Data Analytics Engineer: AED 6K–20K+ per month, tax-free
- AI Engineers: AED 280K–750K+ per year, tax-free
- Target: Analytics Engineer, BI Engineer, AI-powered Analytics Engineer (fintech/banking/consulting)

### Singapore
- Strong Asia-Pacific fintech/analytics hub
- Low tax, high cost-of-living
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, Analytics Product roles

### Canada
- English-speaking, strong analytics demand
- Salaries between UK/EU and US
- Good immigration pathways, stable tech ecosystem
- Target: Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, BI Engineer

### Australia / New Zealand
- English-speaking, good quality of life
- Smaller tech job volumes, growing analytics markets
- Aspirational markets for quality of life

### Bermuda
- Small market, focused on insurance/reinsurance
- Niche analytics roles tied to insurance sector
- Best considered later, only with specific links

### Remote US Startup Market
- Highest upside for remote earnings (~$128K avg, up to $215K for senior data engineers)
- High competition, timezone and company-policy fit matter
- Target: Remote Data Engineer, Analytics Engineer, AI Solutions Engineer, Product Analytics/Tooling Engineer

## 12-Month Phases

| Phase | Months | Focus | Builds Toward |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | 1–3 | Python, pandas, SQL, file handling, cleaning, grouping | Data Analyst / BI Analyst / Reporting Analyst |
| Phase 2 | 4–6 | Databases, joins, BI-style reporting, mini-projects | BI Developer / Reporting Engineer / Junior Analytics Engineer |
| Phase 3 | 7–9 | Cloud, APIs, orchestration, deployment | Analytics Engineer / Data Analytics Engineer / Data Operations Engineer |
| Phase 4 | 10–12 | LLM tools, portfolio polish, security-adjacent topics | AI-powered Analytics Engineer / Data Engineer / AI Solutions Engineer |
